IN consists of pages that can reach the SCC, but cannot be reached from it
SCC a set of nodes (pages) such that for any pair of nodes u and v in the set there is a path from u to v
OUT consists of pages that are accessible from the SCC, but do not link back to it
TENDRILS contain pages that cannot reach the SCC, and cannot be reached from the SCC
TUBE a TENDRIL hanging off from IN to be hooked into a TENDRIL leading into OUT; a passage from a portion of IN to a portion of OUT without touching SCC
DISCONNECTED Fits none of the above
